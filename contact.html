Frequently Asked Questions
Is Nutrivon free to use?
Yes! Nutrivon is free to try via the web app at nutrivon.app with no sign-up required. The mobile app will also be free to download when it launches on App Store and Google Play.
Is Nutrivon a substitute for medical advice?
No. Nutrivon is an informational tool designed to help you understand the nutritional content of food relative to your health conditions. It is not a medical device and does not provide clinical advice. Always consult your doctor or a registered dietitian for medical guidance.
How accurate is the nutritional data?
Nutrivon's AI achieves approximately 94% accuracy in food recognition and nutritional analysis. Data is sourced from reputable nutritional databases and continuously updated. However, exact values may vary based on preparation methods, brands, and portion sizes.
Is my health data private?
Absolutely. We treat your health data with the highest level of confidentiality. We never sell your personal or health data to third parties. Please read our Privacy Policy for full details.
When will the mobile app be available?
We're actively working on the App Store and Google Play releases. In the meantime, you can use the full web app at nutrivon.lovable.app on any device. Follow us for launch announcements.
How do I delete my account and data?
You can request account and data deletion at any time by emailing us at support@nutrivon.app. We will process your request within 30 days in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
