How It Works

Three steps to smarter eating

01

Set your language

Choose your preferred language from 25 supported options. Nutrivon displays every result bilingually — English plus your chosen language simultaneously. Set it once and it's remembered for every future session.

02

Name it, photograph it, or scan it live — then set your health conditions

Type any food name, upload a photo, or use the live camera scan. Then choose from 23 preset health conditions across 5 categories — or add your own.

03

Get your personalized result

See 6 nutrition metrics with daily reference percentages (calories, sugar, fat, salt, fibre, protein), a per-condition suitability rating, health warnings, bilingual AI summary, and practical tips. Download as PDF with food photo and QR code, or share via public link.

Features

Built for your health reality

🧠

AI Nutrition Analysis

Type a food name, upload a photo, or use the live camera scan. Nutrivon's AI delivers a complete nutritional breakdown — calories, macros, sugar, fat, sodium and more — in seconds.

🩺

Condition-Aware Suitability

Every result is filtered through your health conditions. Get a clear suitability rating, health warnings, and guidance specific to you — not generic advice.

📄

PDF Download

Download a beautifully formatted PDF report — includes the food photo, Nutrivon logo, 6 nutrition metrics with daily reference %, all your health condition assessments, bilingual summary, detailed explanation and practical tips. Perfect for sharing with your doctor or dietitian.

🔗

Share Results

Share your result as a public link or export as an image. Links contain only food analysis data — no personal information — and expire after 90 days. Recipients can view the full bilingual result instantly.

⚠️

Health Warnings & Practical Tips

When a food has concerns for your conditions, Nutrivon flags them clearly. Every result includes actionable, bilingual dietary tips to help you make better choices.

🕓

Analysis History

Your last 20 analyses are saved locally on your device for quick reference.

🌐

25 Languages Supported

Choose from 25 languages including English, Chinese, Hindi, Spanish, Malay, Arabic and more. Set a bilingual display — results shown in English plus your chosen language simultaneously.
