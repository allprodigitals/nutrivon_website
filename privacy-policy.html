Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Nutrivon is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our food nutrition and health considerations analysis service.

1 Information We Collect

  • Food names and descriptions you enter for analysis
  • Food images you capture are processed temporarily to generate nutritional insights. Images are not permanently stored unless you choose to save or share your results.
  • Health conditions you select for personalized recommendations
  • Language preferences
  • Device-based local storage of your analysis history (up to 20 recent results)
  • If you create an account: your email address for authentication
  • Usage data including daily and lifetime analysis counts (stored securely on our servers)
  • Subscription status and purchase transaction identifiers (no payment card details are stored by Nutrivon)

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide nutritional analysis and health considerations
  • To generate personalized dietary recommendations based on your selected health conditions
  • To improve our AI analysis quality
  • When you choose to share a result, a public link is created containing only the food analysis data (no personal information). Shared links expire after 90 days and can be deleted at any time.
  • We may collect minimal technical data (such as error logs or performance metrics) to improve app reliability. This data does not include personally identifiable information.

3 Data Storage & Security

  • Your recent analysis history is stored locally on your device for quick access. If you create an account, your analyses and usage data are also stored securely on our servers to sync across sessions.
  • You may use Nutrivon without an account for a limited number of analyses. Creating an account enables full features and syncs your data across sessions.
  • When you share a result, a public link is created. Only food analysis data is included — no personal information. Shared links expire after 90 days and can be deleted at any time.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Nutrivon does not use advertising identifiers, tracking technologies, or third-party analytics to track users across apps or websites.
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect data in transit and at rest

4 Your Rights

  • Clear your local analysis history at any time through your browser/device settings
  • Use the app without creating an account
  • Request deletion of your shared analysis results by contacting us
  • Request a copy of your stored data by contacting us
  • You may request account deletion by contacting us. Upon verification, all associated server-side data will be permanently deleted within a reasonable timeframe.

5 Health Information Disclaimer

⚕️ Nutrivon provides general nutritional information only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.
  • Nutrivon provides general nutritional information only
  • Our analysis is not a substitute for professional medical advice
  • Always consult healthcare professionals for medical decisions
  • We are not responsible for dietary decisions made based on our analysis

6 Children's Privacy

  • Nutrivon is not intended for children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13

7 Subscription Data

  • If you subscribe to Nutrivon Premium, we store your subscription status, plan type, and Apple transaction identifiers to manage your access
  • We do not store or have access to your payment card details — all payments are processed by Apple
  • Subscription data is retained only while your account is active
